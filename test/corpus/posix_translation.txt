==================
Parses a slash-delimited translate command
==================
y/abc/ABC/

---

(script
  (command_list
    (command
      body: (translate_command
        source: (translate_source)
        destination: (translate_destination)))
    (separator)))

==================
Starts another command after a completed translate command
==================
y/abc/ABC/;p

---

(script
  (command_list
    (command
      body: (translate_command
        source: (translate_source)
        destination: (translate_destination)))
    (separator)
    (command
      body: (print_command))
    (separator)))

==================
Parses a translate command with an alternate delimiter

---

(script
  (command_list
    (command
      body: (translate_command

        destination: (translate_destination)))
    (separator)))
